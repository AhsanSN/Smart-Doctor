YPay

PREV NEXT



YPay aims to make the payment process convenient by enabling users to pay through their mobile phone. It works as a virtual banking system where you buy balance from vendors through cash, and then buy anything using this balance. The credit in your account is always interchangeable with money, and thus you can go to any vendor to get cash in exchange for your credits. YPay solves it users' problems of change money, where the vendor usually doesn't have change for your cash. It helps you see all your past transactions, which helps you save your pocket money. This payment method is currently being used by 2 vendors inside Habib University as a medium of payment. All the software and app used by YPay has been created by be using Ionic and PHP. Find the full screenshots on PlayStore.
